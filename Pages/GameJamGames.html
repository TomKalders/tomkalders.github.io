Game Jams

These are some of the game jam projects I have worked on over the years with my group of friends.

Charmageddon Getaway - Winter MelonJam 2023

https://tikinl.itch.io/charmagegeddon

Playable in browser

Charmageddon is a game made for the Winter MelonJam 2023. The theme for this game jam was "Charm". We made a game about a guy who got hold of a shady love potion and became irresistible. This leads into everyone who comes near him to be charmed. The game is an endless runner where you have to not get overwhelmed by the people charmed by you by running through town. You can pick up power-ups to gain health, get a speed boost, drop a decoy or slap people away. You can also use the traffic to your advantage by having people run into upcoming cars.

Mask Up Superstar - Winter MelonJam 2025

https://tkextra.itch.io/mask-up-superstar

Playable in browser

Mask up superstar is a game made for the Winter MelonJam 2025. The theme for this game jam was "Mask". We made a game about the protagonist having to go through a series of minigames to progress through a masked singer style event. The minigames are a guitar hero style minigame where you have to press the buttons in time with the music. Another minigame is a typing minigame where you are being interviewed and have to type out the responses to questions.

The Adventures of BunBun - GMTK 2023

https://tkextra.itch.io/the-adventures-of-bunbun

Playable in browser

The adventures of BunBun was a platformer game made for the Game Jam GMTK 2023 with the theme "Roles Reversed". The idea behind it was to reverse the idea of a platformer by instead of controlling the character you control the environment. You have to guide the bunny through the game by moving platforms, triggering traps and guiding enemies out of the way.

Timelords - MelonJam 5

https://tkextra.itch.io/timelords

Playable in browser

Timelords is a story-driven game made for MelonJam 5 with the theme being "Flow". The player controls the central timeline by making decisions based on events that occur. Based on the decisions, you also gain and lose resources and have to try and balance them all. While the game progresses, the era changes.
